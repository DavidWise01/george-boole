> A sphere of **UD0** — the ROOT0 universe / biosphere. Domain: **LOGIKĒ**.

GEORGE BOOLE (1815–1864) — LOGIKĒ VII, the DESTINATION. Leibniz dreamed logic could be algebra; Boole — a shoemaker's son, self-taught — simply DID IT. Let 1=true, 0=false, and the laws of reasoning become an ALGEBRA: ⚑ AND is MULTIPLICATION (x·y), OR is ADDITION (x+y−xy), NOT is 1−x — reasoning carried out as a SUM (The Laws of Thought, 1854). The single law that makes it logic not arithmetic: x²=x — true only for 0 and 1, so every variable is forced true/false; its twin x(1−x)=0 IS Aristotle's non-contradiction, now a quadratic. ⚑ THE BRIDGE TO THE GATE: in 1937 SHANNON saw a switch is also a 0/1 — series = AND, parallel = OR — so Boolean algebra is the blueprint of every digital circuit. LIVE: (1) toggle x/y → the four ops compute as arithmetic = the truth table (AND=xy verified 1000, OR 1110, XOR 0110); (2) x²=x — y=x and y=x² meet ONLY at 0,1 (verified); (3) a switch circuit — flip A/B, series/parallel, the lamp obeys Boole's arithmetic. CONFIDENCE-TIERED: STRONG (logic IS algebra — AND=×, NOT=1−x, x²=x — exact & foundational, via Shannon the literal basis of every gate) / REFINED-LATER (Boole's OWN 1854 system was quirky — uninterpretable 2x, division; Jevons/Peirce/Schröder filed it to the idempotent lattice x+x=x) / COMPLETED-BY-OTHERS (propositional/class logic only, no quantifiers/relations → Frege next; the circuit link is Shannon's, 70 yrs on; Boole never touched a circuit). Died at 49 (pneumonia, after walking miles in rain to lecture); an 'ingenious curiosity' in life, revealed a century later as the man who wrote the grammar of the machine age. ⚑ The lineage ARRIVES — Boole's algebra is what runs in [[the-branch-predictor]] & all of PSĒPHOS.
